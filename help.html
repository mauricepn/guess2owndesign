HOW TO PLAY THIS GAME ?

in this game , your mission is to guess the secret number hidden under the question mark "?".

FOLLOW THE STEPS BELOW:

1. enter the number between 1 to 20,

2. press ' Enter' or click ' Check' to submit your answer,

3. if your answer matches the hidden ' SECRET NUMBER ', that means you got it right âœ…,

NOTE: you have 20 attempt before the game is over , once you fail for the first try , your score will drop by 1 point, if you keep failing, then you score will eventually drop to 0, therefore you have lost the game.
